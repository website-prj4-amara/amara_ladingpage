WHAT DO WE OFFER?

We at Amara think that becoming a mother is one of the most gratifying but difficult experiences a woman can go through. We are committed to fostering a group of mothers who can unite, exchange stories, and offer one another support as they navigate parenting. We accomplish this, among other things, by planning seminars where mothers can pick up new abilities, increase their knowledge, and meet others who share their interests. Unfortunately, designing these courses can be expensive, so that's where we're asking for your assistance. To generate money for our next workshops, we've started a crowdfunding effort, and we need your help to succeed. You will not only be assisting us in building a dynamic community for mothers by contributing to our campaign, but ou will also receive some incredible benefits in return.
