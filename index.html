WHO ARE WE?

We are a team that focuses on empowering mothers and demolishing the scrutiny they face on a daily basis. We believe that women should be unashamed of the choices that they make and strive to be the most authentic version of themselves. Being a mother can be challenging and demanding, so we want women to not forget to take time for themselves. You can provide the best example for your kids and teach them the value of self-care, by being the best version of yourself.

SUPPORT US

At Amara, we believe in empowering and supporting mothers. One way we achieve this is by creating workshops that bring together mothers from different backgrounds to share their experiences and learn from each other. However, running these workshops requires funds, which is why we're asking for your support through crowdfunding. By donating to our cause, you'll not only help us continue creating valuable workshops, but you'll also become a part of our community of mothers who are dedicated to uplifting and empowering one another. As a thank you for your donation, we'll provide exclusive access to our workshops and events, as well as other special perks. Together, we can create a space where mothers can come together, learn, and grow.
